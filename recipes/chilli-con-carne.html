Chilli Con Carne

We spent quite a while tweaking and adjusting the spice mix for this before settling on the current one. I even like making some extra spice mix to use for tacos or to mix with eggs and mayo.

Ingredients

For about 1kg of meat

Spice Mix

  • 2 tbsp paprika
  • 2 tbsp cumin
  • 1 tbsp oregano
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 to 3/4 tsp cayenne chilli powder
  • 1/2 to 3/4 ancho chilli powder
  • 1/2/ to 3/4 guajillo chilli powder
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp salt

Stewing Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 1 can diced tomato / 1 can of Rotel
  • 1/2 satchel tomato paste / 1 tbsp equivalent
  • 1 tin capiscum
  • 1-2 tsp chipotle in adobo
  • 1 cans of beans
  • 1 litre of beef stock

Method

  1. Combine all the spice ingredients and set aside
  2. In a large pot saute the onion, once translucent add your meat
  3. Once meat is cooked add spice mix, stir for about a minute then add the capisium
  4. After about another minute add the beans, then the tomato, tomato paste and the chipotle.
  5. Finally, add the beef stock, and a little water if you need to cover everything. Cook it down low and slow until the consistence is nice and the sauce is velvety.
