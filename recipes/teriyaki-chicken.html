Teriyaki Chicken

I was really satisfied with this teriyaki chicken, it was easy and quick to make. You can tweak the sauce as you please, we found it a little salty with 2 tbsp soy and 1 tbsp water so it is adjusted as you see in the recipe.

Ingredients

For Meat

  • 500g chicken (cut into about an inch chunks)
  • 2 tbsp sake (for steaming chicken)

For Teriyaki Sauce:

  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp mirin
  • 1 tbsp sake
  • 1 1/2 tbsp soy
  • 1 1/2 tbsp water

Method

  1. Mix teriyaki sauce and put aside for now
  2. Quickly sear 1 side of chiken until slightly golden
  3. Flip chicken and add the sake for steaming, cover with lid
  4. Once sake has cooked down about half, and the bottom side of chicken has some crisp, uncover and add teriyaki sauce
  5. Continue cooking and reduce sauce while spooning over chicken until it becomes thick
  6. Serve over rice with your choice of toppings (spring onions, ontama, furikake, etc.
